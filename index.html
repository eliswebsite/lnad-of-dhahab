Why you should visit

The Land of Dhahab is full of culture, beautiful landscapes and modern cities. Goldville is full of tourist attractions, also containing the best basketball team of all time, the Goldville Lakers. Visiting also supports local businesses, as most of the hotels are run and owned by locals.
