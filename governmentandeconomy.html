The tax rates in The Land of Dhahab are calculated by devotion to the country. Taxes will start with 60% of income plus 5% infrastructure tax. The first 3 years of military service will result in a 15% tax decrease, and each year after will result in a 4% tax decrease. Tax cannot be decreased past 20% but once reached, each year can take 1% off infrastructure tax. Taxes start at age 16.
