Map and Geography of the Land of Dhahab

The Land of Dhahab is full of diverse Geography including vast mountain range and huge rivers. The unique geography provides lots of natural reasources and a self sustaining country. Unfortuanatly with our amazing geography comes risk.The main natural disaster risks are earthquakes, landslides wildfires and floods.
