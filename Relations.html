Allied Nation: Silverville. Relationship: Close

Reason: Both countries believe in the same dictatorial order and value close military alliance.

Nature of the Relationship: Primarily an alliance founded on trade and military cooperation with a focus. They exchange resources and technology with one another and bolster both economies while together against democratic nations.

History: decades back, the two nations were significant mining competitors, competing over control of valuable resources. After a number of border confrontations, a peace agreement was signed, and this led to a strong and smart alliance.
